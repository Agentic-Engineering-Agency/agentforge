# AgentForge Research Orchestrator

Use this skill for `packages/core/src/research/` and CLI or agent features that coordinate research tasks.

## Focus areas

- orchestrator contracts,
- multi-tool sequencing,
- evidence gathering and summarization,
- failure handling when one research source is unavailable.

## Rules

- Preserve source traceability.
- Keep synthesis separate from data collection where possible.
- Prefer explicit orchestration steps over hidden side effects.
